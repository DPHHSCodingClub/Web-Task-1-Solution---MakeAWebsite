The Deliciousness of Pizza

Even pepperoni pizza technically has veggies, since the sauce is made with tomatoes. So when my parents yell at me for eating too much pizza, I tell them I'm just trying to eat my greens!

Ingredients

The Ingredients of pizza include rich marinaria
sauce, juicy red pepperonis, crispy crunchy crust and most importantly,
a good helping of gooey, yummy cheese!!

  • Pizza dough
  • Marinara sauce
  • Pepperonis
  • Cheeeeese

Instructions

  1. Flatten out your pizza dough
  2. Put the marinara sauce on top of your pizza dough
  3. Sprinkly plenty of cheese on top
  4. Carefully lay out your pepperoni
  5. Bake in the oven until crispy!
